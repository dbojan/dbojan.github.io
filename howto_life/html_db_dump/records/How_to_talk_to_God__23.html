How to talk to God?

record index | tag index

tags: God life howto talk

Ask a question, and an answer will come to you.

It is said in the Bible:
Matthew 7:7
"Ask and it will be given to you; seek and you will find; knock and the door will be opened to you."


Note: Sometimes the answer will come before you ask, sometimes later. Maybe even few days later.
The answer will come when you are ready to hear it.

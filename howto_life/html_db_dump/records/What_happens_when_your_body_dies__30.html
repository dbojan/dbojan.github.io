In my opinion:

Your soul goes back to reunite with the God.
Same thing happens with others.
You feel everything you did to others, like you did it to yourself.
So be careful what you do...


Another point of view:
You are the doer, and you are also the subject. Since we are all one...
Connected.

But since soul cannot be damaged, any pain we perceive is ... an illusion, no matter how real it might seem to us.
Served to us to have a new experience, and with such, grow, evolve.
And, it is only temporary.

But, you have a choice. Remember that.

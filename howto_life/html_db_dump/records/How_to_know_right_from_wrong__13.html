"Dr. Mark Powell: How do you know right from wrong?
Prot: Every being in the universe knows right from wrong, Mark."
movie K-PAX (2001)

Listen to that tiny voice inside of you. Listen to your heart.
That is the God speaking to you.

How will you know? You will feel good :) in your heart when you follow your heart.
And you will feel bad when you go astray, or when you act out of fear, or greed.

Always act out of love.
When in doubt, ask you heart.

Compassion is ok. Pity is not.

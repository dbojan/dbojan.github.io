How to overcome shyness?

record index | tag index

tags: shy life howto overcome

Join facebook. You will start to express yourself more freely.
Then join some school or club.

Start talking to people. If you want to ask them something, do it.
Be polite and nice to people. Don't lose your head.
Don't worry too much about your ego, for example don't get too excited if someone insults you, just smile it off.

Don't worry about being perfect, noone expects you to be. :)
So, relax. :)
Do the things you always wanted, experience them (but not to the expense of others.)

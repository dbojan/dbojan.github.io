Don't be afraid to be wrong sometimes. Not intentionally.
It is the part of growing, expanding ideas.
See how an idea "feels". Follow your feelings/emotions.

Don't be afraid to make mistakes.
You will grow (up) through your mistakes.
You will learn much more from mistakes than from successess.
People beliefs change.

Don't discard gut feeling.
Or that fire in your stomach, when you feel something is wrong.
But don't let anyone tell you right from wrong. Use your own head. Use your stomach.

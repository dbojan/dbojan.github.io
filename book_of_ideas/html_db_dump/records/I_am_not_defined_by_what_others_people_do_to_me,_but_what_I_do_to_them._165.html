I am not defined by what others people do to me, but what I do to them.
Not how others people treat you, but how you treat other people.

Think (before you act). They usually have their reasons for acting the way they do.
They and their reasons are mostly unknown to you. Perhaps they feel cornered, or are scared, or are just greedy :) .
Do not judge them, less you be judged.

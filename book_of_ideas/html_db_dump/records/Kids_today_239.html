Kids today

record index | tag index

tags: spiritual hero kid

Kids today have no heroes. They see a bleak future. They see movies in which people who steal and cheat and lie have mansions and lots of girls.
They think that is what it means to succeed in life.

They are forgetting the spiritual side.
They should know, it is important to help other people.
That greed is unnecessary. Fear is unnecessary. There is enough for everyone.

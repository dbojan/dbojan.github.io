Intelligent not necessarily good.
If a race is technologically advanced, it does not necessarily mean they are good to all, and kind.

No matter how much we as a society technologically advance, until we let the love in, it does not mean we are advanced.

Actually it might be just the opposite.
If people in power only think of a new technology as a way to kill more, and more efficiently, it means it has bigger chances of destroying itself, the planet, or both.
We are just a passengers on this planet. We do not own it. It is higher than we are.

If we are not careful, we might have to say some day: "Fools. We had it all, but we lost/ruined it."
(quote from Planet of the apes?, appropriately :) )
Let's hope we get smarter, and get back on the right track.
Let's hope the greed does not get the better of us.
Let the sunshine in.


"Bring us not into temptation, but deliver us from the evil one."

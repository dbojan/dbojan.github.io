Always land on your feet.
There will be times when you are knocked down. It's okay, as long as you get up again.
GET UP.
Prepare yourself to be knocked down sometimes. Program yourself not to be down with the spirit, to get up automatically, when knocked down.

Find a solution to the problem. Eliminate the source, not just the consequence.
Have no fear/ Do not let fear misguide you.
Positive leap forward.

Why do we fear

record index | tag index

tags: possession fear life

Why do we fear? We fear because the thought of losing our possessions, and losing life is unbearable.
Or our body being hurt.
We will have to shed this body at some time.
We don't actually own anything. After we leave this planet, things will not be along with us. Things will not belong to us, just like they never did.

Our life is not ours. It is a gift from God?

Our soul lives forever.

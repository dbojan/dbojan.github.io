fear based world

record index | tag index

tags: anger world fear crash car

We live in a world based on fear, constantly afraid that something of ours is going to get stollen or damaged.
That someone is going to hit our car, that someone is going to ransack our house.
We get angry when we might get, or get, into car accident.
We should not be. Material things by their nature are temporary. They will dissolve in time.
There is no need for fear. No need for anger over losing things, or getting them damaged.
You can't take them to the grave anyway.

Those are valuable lessons anyway. Try to watch yourself in such an event. You might be surprised by yourself. :)
You might learn a lot.

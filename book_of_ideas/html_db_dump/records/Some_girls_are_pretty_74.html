Some girls are pretty

record index | tag index

tags: date girl friend pretty look

Some girls are pretty. Accept that. It does not mean they are perfect, or their life is perfect. Or they make all the right decisions. They make mistakes like everyone else.
Their look is just one aspect of their personality. It's not who they are.
A lot of girls are insecure about their looks. Even the pretty ones.
There will be some girls who will not like you, and who will never go out with you. Live with that.
Some girls will just want you as a friend. You should consider yourself lucky.

You can find beauty in everyone, if you look close enough.

Try to look at them as a person, instead as a sexual prey.
They have dreams, ambitions, ideas, fears.
They have childhood, parents, friends and relatives too. :)
They ARE human beings.

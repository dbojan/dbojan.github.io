How to transfer text to mobile (cell) phone:

Say you need to transfer some text from pc or mac to your mobile phone.

If you don't have it, download qr scanner app on mobile phone. After that is done:

-on your pc/mac, go to http://goqr.me/
-paste text (up to 300 chars)
-on your mobile phone, scan qr image with scanner app. (Live preview)
-you can copy text now using mobile
